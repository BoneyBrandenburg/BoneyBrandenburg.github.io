FullBreathe achieves full human respiration detection by exploiting the complementarity of CSI phase and amplitude of WiFi signals. The system addresses the challenges of phase unwrapping and amplitude variations to provide reliable respiration monitoring.

**Recommended citation:** Y Zeng, D Wu, R Gao, T Gu, D Zhang. (2018). "FullBreathe: Full human respiration detection exploiting complementarity of CSI phase and amplitude of WiFi signals." ACM UbiComp 2018.
