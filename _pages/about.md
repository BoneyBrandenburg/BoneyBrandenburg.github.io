I received my Ph.D. degree from Peking University, advised by Prof. Daqing Zhang (IEEE Fellow). My research focuses on developing novel sensing systems using commodity WiFi devices for human activity recognition, gesture recognition, and health monitoring. I have published multiple papers in top-tier venues including ACM UbiComp, IEEE TMC, and ACM IMWUT.

## Research Interests

- Ubiquitous Computing
- Context Sensing
- Wireless Sensing
- WiFi Sensing
- Human Activity Recognition
- Internet of Things
